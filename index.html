Welcome

This is the official website of the FWF-funded project SlaViCoN: Slavic Verbs in Computational Networks .

SlaViCoN investigates the structure of Slavic verb systems using methods from computational network analysis.

Drawing on large annotated corpora of BCMS and Slovenian, SlaViCoN applies computational network methods to identify structural regularities and to explore broader questions about the nature of morphemes and the relationship between morphology, syntax, and semantics.
